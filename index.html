About

Chien-Wei Huang (黃健崴) is a young designer currently based in Taipei, Taiwan. After a formal education in product design at National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, he is now focusing on consumer electronics and inovation design.

He has been particularly capable of identifying wide-ranged values of designs from the practical to the visionary, meanwhile, also further polished the proficiency on delivering both quality and creativity.

Skill

Professional experience in the following product categories: Consumer electronics, innovation technology, soft goods, bicycle industry, vehicle accessories, sports gear, packaging

Software: Photoshop. Illustrator. Lightroom. Creo. Keyshot. Sketchbook
