Fort

Exspider CR8000 Qi Wireless charging Dashcam

  • Role: Industrial Designer / Product Manager

What if your dashcam didn’t just record your track, but actually connect to your smartphone to sync the data and charge it at the same time? What if it learned your driving habits, helped set alarm, then reminded you with smart, real-time actionable intelligence? Fort is a break new dashcam concept, equipped with Qi wireless charging standard and latest ADAS technology, make your driving experience more smoothly and brilliant.
