Cyclops

Exspider CR5000S Wi-fi Dashcam

  • Role: Industrial Designer
  • Release: 2016 April

Cyclops is the smallest FullHD Wi-Fi dashcam in the world (L52mm x W49mm x H39mm). Equipped with ADAS function, Cyclops not only inherits the sport car feature line but provides a fabulous use experience.
