Robocop

Exspider CR4000 Rear View Mirror Dashcam

  • Role: Industrial Designer

Robocop is a 5” FullHD dashcam merging the rear view mirror with camera function. With the Android os system, police radar detector, GPS module included, Robocop become a mighty full function tech gear. Furthermore, there are two types of design, one designed to replace your current rear view mirror; the other designed to attach by rubber band, which is more convenient to assemble.
